story behind willy enteprises

Willy Enterprise Limited is the construction company based in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. The office of the firm is situated along Nyerere Road adjacent to the Furniture Center in Dar es Salaam region and we also have another strong branchboffice in Arusha region. The company mainly works with construction services as well as supplying some building materials. Its serves wide range of Civil works to Building works without forgetting the mining and drilling works!

These all come to exist and more enough in amazing way, simply because of skilled personnel as well as modern equipment plus our better and friendly working procedures. WEL was incorporated as a limited Company under ordinance (Cap.212) on 22nd January 2003 with Certificate No. 45208. Contractors Registration Board (CRB) registers the firm as CIVIL WORKS CONTRACTORS, class V with registration No.C5/0265/4/04 LOCAL. It is also registered with the same board as BUILDING CONTRACTOR class V with registration No. B5/0192/7/03 LOCAL.
